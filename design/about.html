What is tell-now about?

Secure

We use end-to-end encryption which means that your document leaves your computer encrypted and only gets decrypted once it reaches the receiver's computer.

Easy

No account.
No Download.
No Money.

PGP

Tell-now is a browser-based webapp and uses the open PGP protocol. Tell-now is open-source.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between an https connection and our encryption technology?

An https connection encrypts your communication between your machine and the server. However the company who owns the server (e.g. Google) can read your messages. With tell-now there is no server inbetween and no one can read your documents except you!

I've read that my Gmail is already encrypted, why should I use tell-now?

Because Google can read your emails on the Gmail server. With tell-now, no one except you can access the communication.
